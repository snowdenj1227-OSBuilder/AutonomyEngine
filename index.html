What the Engine ships with

A focused set of systems that turn intent into audited outputs — and real businesses.

  • Prompt → Business

    Compile natural language into AESL plans, scaffold code + tests, wire CI, and ship artifacts with receipts.

    • Coverage gates & SBOM generation
    • Provider-agnostic routing (multi-model)
    • Signed provenance + reproducible builds

  • Freelancer OS

    Proposals, contracts, timelines, invoicing, and handoffs—ready-to-run with audited deliverables.

    • Auto-scoped SOW and acceptance criteria
    • Milestone artifacts + client portal
    • Escrow hooks and receipts

  • Real Estate Agent OS

    Lead funnels, MLS sync, showings, deal rooms, and disclosures with verifiable change history.

    • Auto-generated listings & ad sets
    • Buyer/seller pipelines + tasks
    • Offer workflows with audit trail

  • Student OS

    Research graph, citation engine, study plans, lab notebooks, and collaboration rooms.

    • Source-linked notes & flashcards
    • Problem sets with step-checks
    • Team projects with review gates

  • Healthcare OS soon

    PHI-aware workflows, consent gates, and verifiable audit trails for clinical operators.

    • De-identification & consent checks
    • Tasking with access controls
    • Evidence receipts for every action

  • Ecosystem Marketplace

    Operators, skills, and blueprints—publish, discover, compose, and verify with one click.

    • Versioned skills & reviews
    • One-click compose & test
    • Usage, cost, and reliability stats

  • AE Labs

    Experimental modules & proof kits straight from our research pipeline—opt-in and isolated.

    • Sandboxed runners with budgets
    • Provenance + safety interlocks
    • Public benchmarks
